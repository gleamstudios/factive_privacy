This Privacy Policy explains how Factly ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the App.

We may collect the following types of information:

5. Advertising and Tracking

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. Ads may be personalized based on your activity.

You can control tracking permissions in your device settings.

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with third-party providers only to support app functionality, analytics, and advertising.
